Common Questions

FAQs

How long does ACL rehabilitation take?

ACL reconstruction rehabilitation typically takes 9–12 months before safe return to competitive sport. The first 3 months focus on swelling control, range of motion, and early strength. Full sports-specific training begins around month 6. At VPARC, we use criteria-based milestones at each phase.

Can I continue playing sports with a mild injury?

It depends on the injury. Some conditions worsen with continued play (stress fractures, ligament tears). Others can be managed with load modification and treatment alongside activity. Always get a proper assessment before deciding — VPARC offers same-day appointments.

Is physiotherapy enough for a rotator cuff tear?

For partial tears and many complete tears, physiotherapy is the first-line treatment and achieves excellent outcomes — comparable to surgery in many studies. Full-thickness large tears or those failing conservative management may eventually need surgery, but physiotherapy is always indicated post-operatively.
