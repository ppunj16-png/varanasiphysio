Common Questions

FAQs

How is AI posture analysis different from a standard physiotherapy assessment?

Standard assessments rely on the clinician's eye, which can miss subtle asymmetries and is subject to human error. VPARC's AI-powered system measures 26 parameters with sub-millimetre precision — forward head distance, shoulder height difference, pelvic tilt angle, knee alignment, and more. It provides an objective baseline that can be accurately measured again to track progress.

Can posture analysis help identify the cause of my chronic pain?

Yes — this is the primary clinical purpose of posture analysis at VPARC. Many patients have spent years treating symptoms rather than the underlying postural cause. Our analysis often reveals clear links between postural faults and pain patterns that were never identified in standard examination.

What happens after the posture analysis?

You receive a written clinical report with your 26-parameter measurements and a customised corrective exercise programme with video demonstrations. Most patients also continue with physiotherapy sessions to manually address stiffness and muscle imbalances identified in the report.
