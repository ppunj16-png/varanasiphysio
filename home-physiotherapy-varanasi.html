Overview

Clinic-Quality Care at Your Home

Home physiotherapy is not a compromise — it's a clinically superior option for many patients. Familiar surroundings reduce anxiety, exercises are performed in the actual environment you live in, and family members can be trained alongside.

Full Equipment

Our therapists bring portable electrotherapy units, exercise equipment, and assessment tools — full clinical capability at home.

Same Expert Therapists

Home visits are conducted by the same qualified VPARC physiotherapists who treat patients in the clinic — not support staff.

Flexible Timing

We schedule home visits at times convenient for you and your family, including early mornings and evenings.

Family Training

We teach your family or carer how to safely assist and support your rehabilitation between sessions.

Common Questions

FAQs

Which areas in Varanasi does VPARC cover for home physiotherapy?

VPARC covers all major areas of Varanasi including Sigra, Lanka, BHU area, Rathyatra, Kamachha, Pandeypur, DLW, Bhelupur, Assi, Sunderpur, and most localities within 15 km of the clinic. Call +91 98896 78838 to confirm availability for your specific address.

Is home physiotherapy as effective as clinic physiotherapy?

For many patients — especially post-surgical, neurological, and elderly patients — home physiotherapy is equally or more effective. Exercises performed in your actual living environment improve transfer of functional skills. The main limitation is access to some heavy electrotherapy equipment.

How much does home physiotherapy cost in Varanasi?

Home physiotherapy at VPARC is priced to be accessible. Costs include a home visit charge plus session fees. Contact us at +91 98896 78838 for current rates and package options.
